Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ann Berardocco

Boneless chicken breasts

Italian salad dressing

8 oz. cream cheese

8 oz. Ranch dressing

6-8 oz. hot sauce (i.e., Crystal, not Tobasco)

8 oz. shredded cheddar cheese

Marinate the chicken breasts in the Italian salad dressing and broil until cooked through. Cut the chicken into bite-size pieces.

In a saucepan, mix together the cream cheese, Ranch dressing and hot sauce until melted. Stir in the chicken cubes. Pour the mixture into a greased casserole dish. Sprinkle cheddar cheese on top. Bake in a 350Â° oven until hot and bubbly, 20-30 minutes. Remove from the oven and stir the cheddar cheese into the chicken mix.

Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
