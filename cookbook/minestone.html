Minestrone

The great old favorite! I usually double or triple this recipe when I make it.

1/4 pound salt pork, diced

2 quarts hot water

1 1/2 cups tomato juice

1 can condensed black bean soup

6 beef bouillon cubes

1 small can kidney beans

1 cup carrots, diced

1 cup celery, diced

1 cup turnips (optional in our house)

1 cup cabbage, chopped

1 cup green onions, chopped

1 cup spinach, chopped

1 teaspoon basil

1/2 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon pepper

3/4 cup spaghetti, broken fine

1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated.

Sauté the salt pork in the bottom of a large pot until golden. Add all the ingredients, except the spaghetti and the cheese. Cook at 1 hour, covered. Stir occasionally. Add broken spaghetti. Stir throughout the soup. Simmer another 30 minutes.

Serve hot into soup bowls. Top with parmesan cheese. Accompany with French bread. Freezes well.
