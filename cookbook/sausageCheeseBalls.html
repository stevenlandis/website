Sausage Cheese Balls

Mrs. Newman (Thelma Smith)

Ginny Newman of Cherry Hill and recorder-tootling is not Thelma Smith in disguise. Thelma was one of Ginny's friends.

1 pound cheese (Cooper or Coon)

1 pound raw pork sausage

3 1/2 cups Bisquick

Melt cheese over low heat and add raw pork sausage. Remove from heat. Work in Bisquick and shape into small 1 inch balls. Place on a tray and bake at 350ยบ until brown.
