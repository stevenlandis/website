Waffles

A World of Breads, Dolores Casella

2 cups flour

1 tablespoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon soda

1 teaspoon salt

1-1/2 cups buttermilk, sour milk, cream, milk, yogurt, or sour cream

3 large eggs

1/2 cup melted butter

Sift the dry ingredients and stir in the liquid ingredients, adding the butter last. Beat until smooth. If possible, let the batter stand for several hours before using.

  1. Blueberry waffles--add 1 cup blueberries and 2 tablespoons sugar just before using.
  2. Nut waffles--stir in 1/2 cup or more, of any chopped nuts.
  3. Orange waffles--use 1/2 cup orange juice in place of as much milk, and add grated orange rind. Slivered, blanched, almonds may also be added.
  4. Whole-grain waffles--substitute 3/4 cup cornmeal, rye, or whole-wheat flour, in place of white flour.
