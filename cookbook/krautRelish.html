Kraut Relish

The Hickory House, Wichita, Kansas

This adds to a relish plate, tastes good as a dip with crisp crackers, and highlights a green salad.

1 large (2 1/2) can kraut relish or 2 pound bag

1/2 teaspoon celery seed

1 small can or jar red pimentos, cut in small pieces

2/3 cup sugar

2/3 cup vegetable oil

Mix all the ingredients together. Simmer for 15 minutes after the mixture comes to a boil. Seal in jars while still hot. Serve chilled.
