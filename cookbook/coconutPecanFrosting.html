German Sweet Chocolate Cake

Coconut Pecan Frosting

1 cup evaporated milk

1 cup sugar

3 egg yolks

1/2 cup butter or margarine

1 teaspoon vanilla

1 cup flake coconut

1 cup pecans, chopped

Combine evaporated milk, sugar, egg yolks, butter and vanilla. Cook and stir over medium heat until mixture thickens, about 12 minutes. Add coconut and pecans. Beat until thick enough to spread. Makes 2 1/2 cups.
