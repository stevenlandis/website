Chicken Bouillabase

1 chicken, cut in pieces

3 cups onion, chopped

3/4 cup leeks, cut in small rounds

4 cups tomato (seeded, drained, and chopped)

1/4 cup tomato sauce

5 to 6 garlic cloves, chopped

1 orange peel

1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds

1 teaspoon thyme

2 to 3 bay leaves

Pinch of saffron

Salt and pepper

2 cups white wine

rice

Sweat the chicken pieces by putting them in a skillet with a little hot oil or olive oil for about 10 minutes with the lid on over medium heat. Set aside.

In a Dutch oven or heavy pan, heat 1 tablespoon olive oil or so and sauté the onions and leeks, and garlic. When translucent, add the other ingredients, plus the white wine and the chicken. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer for at least 30 minutes or until done.

Serve chicken bouillabaisse over rice and the following sauce:

1 clove garlic per person

1 teaspoon salt, pounded or blended with the garlic

1 tablespoon basin or oregano

Diced pimento

1/3 cup bread crumbs

1 egg yolk

The easy method to make the sauce is to put the sauce ingredients in a blender, and slowly add oil to liquefy the sauce to make it spreadable. Don't forget to add a hot pepper or hot pepper flakes.
