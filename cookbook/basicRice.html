PAUL PRUDOMME’S BASIC RICE

Paul Prudomme via Ann Reid

This is a very good full-flavored rice dish to serve with gumbo and other delights from the New Orleans region.

Preheat oven to 350°.

2 cups rice, raw

2-1/2 cups chicken stock

1-1/2 tablespoons onions, finely chopped

1-1/2 tablespoons celery, finely chopped

1-1/2 tablespoons bell pepper, finely chopped

1-1/2 tablespoons melted butter

½ teaspoon salt

1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

pinch each of white pepper, cayenne, and black pepper

Combine all the ingredients above in a bowl. After mixing well, pour mixture into a greased 5” x 9” x 2-1/2 –inch loaf pan. Seal top with foil and bake in a 350° oven for 70 minutes.

Transfer to a serving bowl and serve. Chopped parsley could be sprinkled on top of the rice. This recipe makes about 6 cups of cooked rice.
