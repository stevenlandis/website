Crabbies

Barbara DuPuy

Oven: 350ยบ

1 small jar Old English cheese, sharp

1 stick butter

1 can crab meat or 8 oz. fresh, steamed

1/2 t. garlic powder

1 T. mayonnaise

6 English muffins

Cream the cheese, butter, crab, garlic powder, and mayonnaise. Split the muffins. Spread each half with the crab mixture. Cut in quarters. Bake on an ungreased cookie sheet about 15 minutes or until bubbly. Turn on broiler and broil briefly until brown.
